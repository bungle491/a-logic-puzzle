The Spinning Table

A dark room. A spinning table. Four coins. Can you align them all without seeing?

The rules

A cylindrical table has cup holders equally spaced around its edge. Each cup holds a coin — heads or tails — but the room is pitch dark. You can only feel the coins, not see them.

Each move: reach into two cups simultaneously (adjacent or diagonal in the 4-cup game, any four in the 8-cup game), feel what's inside, then flip whichever coins you choose. Then the table spins by an unknown amount — you lose all sense of which cups you just touched.

Goal: get all coins to the same face — all heads or all tails — in as few moves as possible. The proven minimum for the 4-cup puzzle is 5 moves.
